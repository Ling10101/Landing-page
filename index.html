About the Festival


The World Halal Food Festival is an annual event celebrating the rich diversity of halal cuisine and culture in London. Featuring over 150 vendors offering halal dishes from around the world, the festival also includes a Live Cookery Theatre, VIP Lounge, Kids Zone, and a Live Stage with performances and entertainment. Open to all, it's a welcoming space where food lovers can explore new flavours, enjoy live experiences, and connect with the community. Join us for a weekend of delicious food, family fun, and cultural discovery!


28th - 29th September 2024


London Stadium, Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park, E20
